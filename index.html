HOVER NEWS & QUOTES

BY MICHAEL ON OCTOBER 18, 2006

Right under the navigation menu to the right you will see a heading called Recent Updates. If you hover over the news items a short summary will pop up and allow you to click to get to another page. you can use this as a link list giving people a short description of waht your link is about. I learned the technique from Stu at CSS Play. The original demo was to display a small photo gallery this way but i adapted it for text.
